# 统计学(Stat)
全称Statistical functions
**norm**

$$
f(x) = \frac{exp(-x^2/2)}{\sqrt{2\pi}}
$$

**lognorm**

$$
f(x,s) = \frac{1}{sx\sqrt(2\pi)}exp(-\frac{(log)^2(x)}{2s^2})
$$
